# Strategos — Professional Forensic Suite

> *Strategos Autokrator* — Supreme Commander. The title of Philip II and Alexander the Great.

Strategos is a suite of professional tools for digital forensic investigations — built for practitioners, designed for court-ready casework.

---

## Suite Overview

| Product | Package | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **Nika** | `niki` | Forensic parser — full spectrum evidence ingestion |
| **Komanda** | `komanda` | Case management |
| **Niska** | `niska` | Collaborative investigation review |
| **Sila** | `sila` | Evidence management with immutable chain of custody |
| **Vistina** | `vistina` | Legal review and forensic-to-legal production |

---

## Architecture

```
Strategos Suite
│
├── Nika          — Ingest & parse evidence (iOS, Android, macOS)
│       ↓
├── Komanda       — Manage cases, assign investigators, track status
│       ↓
├── Niska         — Review evidence collaboratively across the case team
│       ↓
├── Sila          — Manage exhibits, maintain chain of custody
│       ↓
└── Vistina       — Legal review, redaction, production to counsel
```

The suite follows evidence through its full lifecycle — from raw extraction to legal production.

## Naming

All five system names are Macedonian. They form a cohesive naming family and are phonetically accessible to English speakers.

| Product | Macedonian Meaning | Pronunciation |
|---|---|---|
| **Nika** | Victory | NEE-ka |
| **Komanda** | Command | koh-MAN-da |
| **Niska** | Thread | NEES-ka |
| **Sila** | Force, power | SEE-la |
| **Vistina** | Truth | VEES-tee-nah |

Niska is inspired by the Vardar river — the thread that flows through the heart of North Macedonia, and the connective tissue between Nika and Vistina in the suite.
